Smart Home Remodeling: Your Trusted Houston General Contractor

Smart Home Remodeling has been transforming homes across Houston since 2010, offering expert general contracting services from kitchen remodels to emergency roof repairs. Our team delivers quality craftsmanship, on-time delivery, and exceptional customer care.

About Smart Home Remodeling

Smart Home Remodeling is Houston’s trusted general contractor, dedicated to transforming homes into beautiful, functional spaces. With over 15 years of experience, our licensed technicians bring craftsmanship, innovation, and a commitment to quality to every project—whether it’s a full kitchen remodel, a basement finish, or a new roof.

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by a team of seasoned builders, Smart Home Remodeling began as a small family‑owned shop focused on residential renovations. As word spread about our meticulous attention to detail and transparent pricing, we grew into a full‑service contractor capable of handling projects from modest updates to expansive additions. Today, we’re proud to serve Houston and its surrounding communities, maintaining the same passion for excellence that launched us.
