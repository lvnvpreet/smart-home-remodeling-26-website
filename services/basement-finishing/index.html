Prevention Tips

  • Regular Inspections – Check for cracks, water stains, and mold growth at least twice a year.
  • Maintain Proper Ventilation – Use dehumidifiers and exhaust fans to keep humidity low.
  • Seal Windows & Doors – Apply weatherstripping to prevent moisture infiltration.
  • Clear Gutters & Downspouts – Ensure water flows away from the foundation.
  • Install Sump Pumps – Protect against flooding during heavy rain or snow melt.
